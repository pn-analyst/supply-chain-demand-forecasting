# Supply Chain Demand Forecasting Dashboard — Product A (2021–2024)

This project presents a complete demand forecasting dashboard built in Power BI using historical monthly demand data for Product A from 2021 to 2023, with a forecast extending into 2024. The dashboard supports supply chain planning, inventory optimization, and strategic decision‑making.

## Key Insights

- Last 12 Months Demand: 7,075  
- Forecast Next 12 Months: 9,436  
- Forecast Growth: 33.4%  
- Total Demand (2021–2024): 20,185  
- Average Monthly Demand: 760  

These KPIs provide a clear snapshot of historical performance and expected future demand.

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Actual & Forecasted Demand Over Time
- Historical demand from Jan 2021 to Dec 2023  
- Forecast begins Jan 2024  
- Highlights long‑term trends and seasonality  
- Supports planning for production, procurement, and logistics  

### 2. Cumulative Demand Over Time
- Running total from Jan 2021 to Dec 2024  
- Shows long‑term growth trajectory  
- Useful for capacity planning and annual budgeting  

### 3. Monthly Demand Comparison by Year
- Year‑over‑year comparison for 2021, 2022, and 2023  
- Highlights seasonal peaks and dips  
- Shows consistent growth trend  

### 4. Monthly Demand Breakdown Table

| YearMonth | Sum of Demand |

| 2023‑12 | 760 |

| 2023‑09 | 585 |

Enhancements applied:
- Conditional formatting to highlight peak months

### 5. Interactive Month Selection
- Buttons for each month (Jan–Dec)  
- Allows users to filter visuals instantly  
- Improves usability and exploration
